Internet of Things

IoT transforms secure poultry management by deploying sensors in chicken farms to monitor key variables like temperature, humidity, and air quality in real-time.These devices continuously observe the farm environment, providing instant and accurate information to farmers. By seamlessly integrating IoT, chicken farms leverage real-time information from monitoring devices, empowering farmers to make data-driven decisions for optimal poultry health and secure management.

IPFS

The utilization of the InterPlanetary File System (IPFS) offers a cost-effective and secure solution for data storage. As IoT sensors generate a plethora of real-time information in chicken farms, IPFS provides a decentralized and efficient platform to store this data. By leveraging IPFS, the system ensures data integrity and accessibility for authorized users, such as farm administrators.

Blockchain

Blockchain enhances secure poultry management by providing an immutable and transparent ledger for recording critical farm data. In chicken farms, blockchain ensures the integrity of information related to health records, environmental conditions, and supply chain processes. This technology improves traceability, accountability, and overall security in poultry management by creating a trustworthy and decentralized record-keeping system.
